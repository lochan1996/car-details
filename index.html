overview

The Hyundai i20 Elite Automatic is a premium level hatchback from Hyundai which has proven to be a market winner in all rights. The Hyundai i20 Automatic has an attractive Fluidic design and is priced at INR 9,02,000 (ex-showroom Delhi). It comes with a 1.4 litre engine which gives out a maximum power of 100 PS @ 6000 rpm and a maximum torque of 132 Nm @ 4000 rpm. The car has a ground clearance of 170 mm and a boot space of 295 litres. In the premium segment i20 Elite is a competitor to the likes of Maruti Suzuki Baleno.
